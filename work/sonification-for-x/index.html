David Leith :: Work :: Sonification for X

Image by Sonic Darts

Track featured on the Resonance FM show Gwaith Sŵn’s Sonic Darts in February 2024.

This composition started as an investigation of compositional styles of several classical and modern electronic composers. The initial concept was to create work using a minimum of very different but contrasting electronic sounds. After listening it reminded me of the emotional roller coaster a person might encounter.
