David Leith :: Work :: Before and After the Protest (Pancras Station)

Remixed field recording for Cities and Memory - remixing the world.

London protest reimagined.

“The musical theme is based on the powerful cadence of the speakers voice at the Kurdistan protest at Pancras station in London England. The composition includes fragments of the every”day to day" hustle and bustle, to the tension of the protest and the lingering memories resonating throughout the space. "– Excerpt from the project site –
